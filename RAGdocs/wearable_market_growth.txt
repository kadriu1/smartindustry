Title: Wearable Technology & Health Tracking Growth Overview

This document summarizes key growth statistics for wearable health and wellness technology.

Key Points:
- The wearable health monitoring market continues to grow rapidly.
- Continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) is one of the fastest growing segments.
- CGM market projected to rise from 15.3 billion USD in 2026 to 31.4 billion USD by 2031.
- Growth driven by increasing demand for real-time health data and advanced sensor technology.
- Users are shifting from fitness-only trackers to holistic health monitoring devices.

Title: Wellness Technology Market Size and Trends

Summary of the broader wellness technology market.

Key Points:
- Estimated market size expected to rise from roughly 65 billion USD (2026) to over 208 billion USD by 2035.
- Wearable devices represent about 48 percent of the sector's revenue.
- Continued growth in digital mental wellness tools and holistic monitoring technologies.
- Consumer demand focuses on actionable insights and personalized health data.

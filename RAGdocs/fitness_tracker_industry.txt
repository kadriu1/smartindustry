Title: Wearable Fitness Tracker Industry Trends

Industry-level perspective on competitive developments in wearable fitness trackers.

Key Points:
- Fitness trackers now integrate clinical-grade features like ECG and blood oxygen measurement.
- Manufacturers expanding ecosystems with apps and cloud platforms for long-term health insights.
- Competition increasing globally, with strong growth in Asia-Pacific markets.
- Innovation driven by low-power sensors and continuous monitoring capabilities.
